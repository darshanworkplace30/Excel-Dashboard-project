# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

**Brewing up some serious data!** 📊

Welcome to my Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard project. This repository contains a comprehensive interactive Excel dashboard visualizing nearly $700,000 in total sales and over 149,000 customer visits across multiple store locations.

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* **⏰ Morning Rush:** The 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM window clearly drives the most significant order volume.
* **☕ Customer Favorites:** Coffee dominates at 39% of total sales, followed by Tea at 28%. The "Barista Espresso" stands out as the top revenue generator.
* **📍 Store Showdown:** Hell's Kitchen took a slight lead in total sales (over $236K), but Astoria and Lower Manhattan are highly competitive.
* **💰 Consistent Value:** The average bill sits steadily at $4.69 per person across all three locations.

## 🛠️ Behind the Build & Methodology

A huge shoutout to an excellent YouTube tutorial that provided the raw dataset and the core layout inspiration for this project. Following along was an incredible way to master advanced Excel techniques!

**Skills & Tools Highlighted:**
* **Power Pivot:** Used for advanced data modeling and connecting multiple data tables.
* **Custom Measures:** Wrote dynamic custom measures for accurate KPI calculations (Total Sales, Footfall, Avg. Bill).
* **Data Visualization:** Applied highly effective formatting and charting tricks to create a clean, presentation-ready format.

Building this from scratch was a fantastic exercise in transforming raw transactions into a structured, business-ready format.
